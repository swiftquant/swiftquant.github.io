Company Profile

Company background

JQ Securities (Hong Kong) Limited (Former Name: Thor Asset Management Limited) (“the Company”) is a company incorporated in Hong Kong in 2017, which is a financial institution licensed with the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission to carry out regulated activities including dealing in securities (Type 1), advising on securities (type 4) & asset management (type 9). The company obtained the Exchange Participant of the Hong Kong Stock Exchange and officially launched its regulated business in February 2019. The company is committed providing quality financial services to customers.

The company provides a comprehensive range of products and services, including

  • Securities Brokerage service
  • Asset Management
  • Investment Advisory
  • Corporate and Personal investment services

Striving for the upper reaches of the enterprise spirit, macro vision of the global market, strict internal control system, and trustworthy customer relationships are the company's core values. The company's goal is to build a fully functional financial platform to provide customers with low-cost solutions to achieve their investment goals.

The company is composed of licensed persons from the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong. The company has a talented and capable team that has become the strongest service team for the company's operations. The company is good at understanding customer needs and tailormade services for the interests of customers.
