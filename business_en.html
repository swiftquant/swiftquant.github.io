Business

Bond Project

Securities Research and Asset Management

The company provides securities brokerage services for retail investors and high net worth individuals. The company is an exchange participant (participant ID: 02140) of The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited ("Stock Exchange") and a direct clearing participant of Hong Kong Securities Clearing Company Limited. The securities brokerage services provided by the company include all stocks, debt securities and exchange-traded funds listed on the Main Board and Growth Enterprise Market of the Stock Exchange.


Securities Research and Asset Management

The company will issue research reports to retail investors and high-net-worth individuals in Hong Kong and Mainland China, recommending the purchase or sale of stocks and debt securities listed on the Stock Exchange. The company will provide discretionary management services for managed accounts and offshore hedge funds in the Cayman Islands.


Development

The company intends to provide customers with investment consulting and bond brokerage investment services in the near future. With the rapid expansion of the Chinese bond market in the past decade and becoming one of the world's largest bond markets, the company aims to develop bond brokerage business and conduct on- and off-exchange bond transactions on behalf of investors. By looking for bond investment channels in the primary and secondary markets in the Greater China region, the company strives to provide customers with opportunities to earn fixed and stable income in a rapidly changing environment. The company's potential customers are high-net-worth companies and individual professional investors.


Customers and markets

Customers come from the personal/business network of the company's senior management, and it is expected that most of the customers will be residents and corporate customers in Hong Kong and China. The company's business development is mainly introduced through recommendations, and there is no aggressive marketing strategy.
