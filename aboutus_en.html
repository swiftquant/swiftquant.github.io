About Us

Zhenjiang State-owned Investment Holding Group Co., Ltd. ("the Group") was established in June 2014. The Group was formed by integrating the assets (equity) of state-owned enterprises affiliated to the municipal and relevant departments on the basis of the former Zhenjiang State-owned Assets Investment and Operation Company. The registered capital of the Group is 1 billion yuan and the Group has three main business segments: industry, finance, and asset management. The Group has 26 second-level wholly-owned subsidiaries, 30 second-level shareholding companies, and approximately 8,000 employees. As of the end of 2019, the Group's total assets were 58 billion yuan, net assets were 24.5 billion yuan, and consolidated operating income exceeded 10 billion yuan.

Since the establishment of the Group, in accordance with the principle of “similar businesses, same functions, and complementary advantages”, it has promoted the optimization and integration of group assets and enterprises and functional reorganization, and has strived to create a fully functional and efficient local financial holding platform and industrial development platform. Good development momentum. The financial sector has formed four major types of businesses: fund investment, direct equity investment, financial services, and equity participation in financial institutions. The scale of financial assets is about 5 billion yuan. The Group invest in 10 listed companies (4 on the Main Board and 6 on the New Third Board) and the layout of the physical industry sector covers food manufacturing, automobiles and parts, light industry, new chemical materials, engineering construction, and bulk commodity trade. The influence, driving force and control of the Group have been significantly enhanced.

The Group adheres to the principle of seeking progress while maintaining stability, maintaining strength, consolidating the foundation, seeking truth and being pragmatic, and unswervingly advancing "marketization, specialization, and entity" in accordance with the overall idea of "reducing debt, adjusting structure, flexing mechanism, and generating benefits" It strives to promote the establishment of a management system and operating mechanism that meets the requirements of high-quality development, and is committed to becoming an important backbone of an industrial city, striving to become a first-class state-owned capital investment and operation group in the province.
